About Us

Leading the industry with quality, innovation, and reliability

Excellence in Fencing Manufacturing

New India Fencing Industry (NIFI) has established itself as a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-quality fencing solutions across India. With years of expertise in the industry, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and installing a comprehensive range of fencing products that meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, innovative designs, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for residential, commercial, and industrial fencing projects. We use only the finest materials and employ cutting-edge manufacturing techniques to ensure durability, security, and aesthetic appeal in every product we deliver.

At NIFI, we understand that every project is unique. Our team of experienced professionals works closely with clients to provide customized solutions that perfectly match their requirements, budget, and timeline.
